Kunefe

Ingredients

  • Kadaif
  • Turkish string cheese
  • Unsalted butter
  • Pistachio

Steps

  1. First prepare the sugar syrup
  2. Place kadafi in a food processor and chop up finely.
  3. Add butter and milk and line a 8 inch square heavy baking dish with half of this mixture.
  4. Mix the cheeses with the pistachios, sugar& nutmeg and spread onto shredded filo.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes.
  6. Make a sugar syrup by boiling water with sugar and lemon juice.
  7. When kunefe comes out of the oven, ladle 2/3 of the hot syrup over it and save the rest to pass while eating.
  8. Enjoy!
